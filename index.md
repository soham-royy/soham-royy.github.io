## About Me

I am **Soham Roy**, Regional Manager at **Sadhana Forest Immersion Centers of Meghalaya** and Project Director at the **Sadhana Forest Jaintia Hills Immersion Center**, based in Moolamylliang, East Jaintia Hills, Meghalaya.

My academic and professional interests lie at the intersection of **social ecology, ecology and evolution, conservation biology, wildlife ecology, climate change and sustainability, environmental management, plant biology, and biostatistics**. I completed my **Bachelor of Science in Zoology** from **Bankura Sammilani College, Bankura University**, with a CGPA of **8.10**.

My current work connects ecological restoration, community engagement, field monitoring, and environmental management across Meghalaya. I am especially interested in conservation and restoration questions where field ecology, data analysis, and local community action come together.

## Research Interests

- Social ecology and community-based ecological restoration
- Ecology and evolution
- Conservation biology and wildlife ecology
- Climate change, sustainability, and environmental management
- Plant biology and habitat restoration
- Biostatistics and ecological data analysis
