## Research & Projects

My research interests are centered on **social ecology, ecology and evolution, conservation biology, wildlife ecology, climate change and sustainability, environmental management, plant biology, and biostatistics**. My work combines field-based ecological observation, community engagement, environmental assessment, and biological laboratory methods.

## Research Interests

- **Social ecology:** community-centered ecological restoration and environmental action
- **Ecology and evolution:** species-habitat relationships, biodiversity, and ecological processes
- **Conservation biology and wildlife ecology:** bird ecology, wetland habitats, and field-based monitoring
- **Climate change and sustainability:** environmental management and restoration in changing landscapes
- **Plant biology and habitat restoration:** vegetation, habitat recovery, and restoration-oriented field assessment
- **Biostatistics:** use of statistical and computational tools for biological and ecological data

## Selected Academic Projects

### Nest and Nesting Behaviour of Asian Openbill Stork (*Anastomus oscitans*)

A field-based ecological study on nesting behaviour and habitat use at Barachaka wetland, West Bengal. This project formed part of the foundational work preceding a later peer-reviewed publication on Asian openbill stork abundance and nesting habits.

### *Mycobacterium tuberculosis* as a Vertebrate Parasite

A systematic literature review on the morphology, genome organization, drug resistance, and pathophysiology of *Mycobacterium tuberculosis*.

### Morphological Studies of Various Castes of *Apis* sp.

An illustrated morphological and anatomical review of honey bee castes, with comparative analysis of external and internal structures.
